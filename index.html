Executive Summary

This data science initiative analyzed over six decades of FEMA Natural Disasters data, transforming raw government declarations into a structured analytical framework. The objective: map the trajectory of American disaster landscapes through temporal escalation, geographic risk profiles, and federal response mechanisms.

Temporal Pattern
Escalating Trend
Significant statistical increase in declarations over time. 2005 remains the absolute peak in recorded history.
Vulnerability Zones
TX & CA
Texas and California dominate historical records, displaying highly distinct geographical threat signatures.
Federal Mechanics
Infrastructure First
Public Assistance (PA) outpaces Individual Housing aid, positioning the government primarily as an infrastructure restorer.
